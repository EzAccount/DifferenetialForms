 	Set G with binary operation $*: G \times G \to G$ is called a \textit{group} $(G, *)$ if:
 	\begin{enumerate}
 		\item $\forall a,b,c \in G: a*(b*c) = (a*b)*c $
 		\item $\exists e \in G: \forall a \in G, a*e=e*a=a$
 		\item $\forall a \in G, \exists b\in G: a*b = e$
 	\end{enumerate}
